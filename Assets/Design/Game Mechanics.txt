Game Mechanics

# The player can create a character
# The player can control a character
# The player can change the equipment of a character
# Characters have a starting class
# Characters can move around (8 dir)
#	Movement is not restricted to tiles
# Characters can attack
# Characters can take damage
# Characters can take damage over time
# Characters can heal damage
# Characters can heal damage over time
# Characters can loot items
# Characters have stats
#	health, max health
# Characters have a name
# Characters have equipment slots

# The world is made up of tiles
# Tiles are walkable or blocking movement
